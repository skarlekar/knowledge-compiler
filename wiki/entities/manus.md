# Manus

## Overview

Manus was an agentic AI startup acquired by Meta in December 2025 for approximately $2 billion. The acquisition is cited as the clearest market signal that the competitive moat in agentic AI lies in the harness — the orchestration infrastructure — not in the underlying model. Manus used foundation models from Anthropic, OpenAI, and others — the same models any team can access via API. Meta bought the harness.

## Characteristics

- **Founded**: Prior to 2025 (exact date not specified in source)
- **Acquired by**: Meta, December 2025
- **Acquisition price**: ~$2 billion
- **Model approach**: API-based; used Anthropic, OpenAI, and other foundation models — not a model company
- **Core value**: Agentic orchestration infrastructure — solving "the messy, unglamorous & highly complex problem of agentic orchestration that turns AI from running Chatbots into an autonomous digital worker"
- **Signal**: The acquisition price for a company with no proprietary model is the market's valuation of harness infrastructure

## Common Strategies

- [Harness Engineering](../concepts/harness-engineering.md) — Manus's product was the harness; the acquisition validates harness engineering as the durable competitive advantage in agentic AI
