# Andrej Karpathy

## Overview

Andrej Karpathy is an AI researcher, educator, and former Director of AI at Tesla and founding member of OpenAI. He coined the term "[vibe coding](../concepts/vibe-coding.md)" in February 2025, naming the intuition-driven, no-spec approach to AI-assisted development that dominated early agent adoption.

## Characteristics

- **Known for**: Foundational work in deep learning and computer vision; AI education (Andrej's neural network courses are widely referenced); coining "vibe coding"
- **Vibe coding quote** (February 2025): Called it "the hottest new programming paradigm" while acknowledging it works best for throwaway projects where correctness doesn't matter
- **X/Twitter**: https://x.com/karpathy/status/1886192184808149159 (original vibe coding post)

## Common Strategies

- [Vibe Coding](../concepts/vibe-coding.md) — coined and popularized the term that named Phase 1 of the [agent maturity arc](../concepts/agent-maturity-arc.md)
